About Me

My name is Montajab and I study Law at the American University of Iraq, Sulaimaniyah (AUIS). My main focus as a law student encompasses learning the different angles of constitutional and international law, centralizing my interest in aviation law. Public speaking and legal education are my two main strengths, and my vision is to provide clear and direct content in forms of videos and essays of the laws for the general public to get to grips with. Besides my academics, I am a sales representative at iLens which is the lead production company of 360 virtual reality tours in Iraq.
